Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about PayFlow pricing.

What payment methods do you support?
PayFlow supports all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay), bank transfers, and over 50 local payment methods globally.
Can I change plans later?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged the prorated difference for the remainder of your billing cycle. When downgrading, the change will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
Is there a contract or long-term commitment?
No, all plans are month-to-month with no long-term contract required. You can cancel at any time. Enterprise plans may have custom contract terms based on specific requirements.
How does the free trial work?
The free trial gives you full access to the Professional plan features for 14 days. No credit card is required to start. At the end of the trial, you can choose to continue with any plan or stick with the free plan.
What happens if I exceed my transaction limit?
If you exceed your monthly transaction limit, we'll automatically charge you for the additional transactions at your plan's standard rate. You'll receive a notification when you're approaching your limit so you can upgrade if needed.
Do you offer discounts for non-profits?
Yes, we offer special pricing for registered non-profit organizations. Please contact our sales team with proof of your non-profit status to learn about our discounted rates.
