Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about PayFlow support.

What are your support hours?
Email support is available 24/7, with typical response times of 2 hours during business hours (9 AM - 6 PM EST, Monday-Friday). Live chat is available during business hours. Phone support is available for Enterprise customers 24/7.
How do I check the status of my support ticket?
After submitting a support request, you'll receive a confirmation email with a ticket number. You can reply to that email thread at any time for updates, or log into your PayFlow account and visit the Support section to view all your open tickets.
Do you offer phone support?
Phone support is available for Enterprise plan customers 24/7. For Professional plan customers, phone support is available during business hours. Starter and Free plan customers have access to email and chat support during business hours.
What information should I include when contacting support?
To help us resolve your issue quickly, please include: your account email, a detailed description of the issue, steps to reproduce the problem, relevant error messages, and any screenshots or logs that might be helpful. For technical issues, include your integration method and code snippets if applicable.
How can I get help with integration issues?
For integration help, we recommend checking our comprehensive developer documentation first. If you're still stuck, our support team can help with specific implementation questions. For complex integration projects, we offer dedicated technical account management for Enterprise customers.

Still need help?

Our support team is ready to assist you with any questions or issues.
